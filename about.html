About StockWatch

StockWatch is a comprehensive financial market tracking application designed to provide real-time data on stocks, cryptocurrencies, and currency exchange rates. Our mission is to make market information accessible and understandable for everyone.

The application was developed with a focus on user experience, providing a clean, intuitive interface that resembles the iOS stock app but with enhanced features and functionality.

Features

  • Real-time stock market data
  • Cryptocurrency price tracking
  • Currency converter with African currencies support
  • Financial news aggregation
  • Market predictions and analysis
